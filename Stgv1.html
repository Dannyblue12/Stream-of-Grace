About Us

Stream of Grace Chapel Int'l is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to experiencing the presence of God through worship, prayer, and fellowship. Founded in 2010, our ministry has grown from a small gathering to a thriving congregation that touches lives across the region.

Our mission is to create an atmosphere where people can encounter God's presence in a powerful and transformative way. We believe that in His presence, lives are changed, hearts are healed, and destinies are fulfilled.

Through our worship services, outreach programs, and community initiatives, we strive to be a beacon of hope and a channel of God's grace to all who come through our doors.
