Our Church

Located in Mallam (near Socca Bet) Accra, Ghana, Stream of Grace Chapel International is a vibrant and dynamic Christian community dedicated to spreading the message of God's love and redemption. We welcome everyone to join us in worship and fellowship as we grow together in faith.

Discover Our Purpose

Our Mission

Founded on the principles of faith, hope and charity, our church seeks to provide a spiritual haven for individuals and families from all walks of life. Under the visionary leadership of Pastor Peggy Lipton, we strive to empower believers to live victorious lives, built on the solid foundation of God's Word.
