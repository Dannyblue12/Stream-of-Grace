Join Us in Worship

Weekly Services

Join us for our regular weekly services designed to help you grow in your relationship with God.

Sunday Bootcamp Service

Every Sunday, 8:30 - 10:30 AM

Our Sunday Bootcamp Service is the main gathering of our church family. It's a time of vibrant worship, powerful preaching, and experiencing the presence of God. Pastor Peggy Lipton delivers anointed messages that are both inspiring and practical for everyday living. The service includes a time of praise and worship, prayer, offering, and ministry.

Worship Experience

Our worship team leads the congregation in a powerful time of praise and worship, creating an atmosphere for God's presence.

Word of God

Pastor Peggy delivers anointed messages that are relevant, practical, and life-changing, helping believers grow in their faith.

Children's Church

We provide age-appropriate teaching and activities for children during the main service, helping them grow in their understanding of God.

Ministry Time

After the message, there is a time of ministry where people can receive prayer for healing, deliverance, and other needs.

Rhema Service

Every Thursday, 5:30 - 7:30 AM

Our Rhema Service is a midweek gathering focused on in-depth Bible study and spiritual growth. This early morning service provides deeper insights into God's Word and practical applications for daily living. It's a time of refreshing and equipping believers to live victorious lives throughout the week.

Bible Study

In-depth exploration of Scripture, helping believers gain a deeper understanding of God's Word and its application to daily life.

Spiritual Growth

Focused teaching on spiritual disciplines, character development, and growing in relationship with God.

Encouragement

A time of encouragement and motivation to help believers face the challenges of the week with faith and confidence.

Prophetic Ministry

Opportunity to receive prophetic insights and guidance through Pastor Peggy's prophetic gift.

Prayer Chain

First Saturday of Every Month

Our Prayer Chain is a dedicated time of corporate prayer where we intercede for our church, community, nation, and world. It's a powerful gathering where believers come together to seek God's face and pray for breakthrough in various areas. This monthly prayer meeting is open to all who desire to join in united prayer.

Intercessory Prayer

Focused prayer for specific needs, situations, and people, standing in the gap on behalf of others.

Community Prayer

Prayer for our local community, addressing specific needs, challenges, and opportunities for ministry.

Global Impact

Prayer for nations, global issues, missionaries, and the advancement of God's kingdom worldwide.

Personal Needs

Opportunity to receive prayer for personal needs, healing, deliverance, and breakthrough in various areas of life.
