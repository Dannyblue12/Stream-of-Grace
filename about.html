Our Pastor

Meet the visionary leader behind Stream of Grace Chapel International

Pastor Peggy Lipton

Lead Pastor, Prophetess, Conference Speaker

Pastor Peggy Lipton is a dynamic and anointed prophetess, pastor, and conference speaker, dedicated to spreading the word of God and empowering believers worldwide. As the Lead Pastor of Stream of Grace Chapel International, she passionately serves her congregation and community with love, wisdom, and spiritual guidance.

With a strong online presence, Pastor Peggy Lipton connects with thousands through her social media platforms (Pastor Peggy Lipton on all platforms), sharing inspiring messages, prophetic insights, and practical teachings. Her ministry is marked by a deep commitment to spiritual growth, healing, and transformation.

Through her prophetic gift, Pastor Peggy Lipton brings hope, encouragement, and clarity to individuals and communities, helping them navigate life's challenges and discover their purpose. As a sought-after conference speaker, she has ministered to diverse audiences, igniting revival and awakening in the hearts of many.

Under her leadership, Stream of Grace Chapel International has become a beacon of hope and a hub for spiritual growth, where people from all walks of life find refuge, support, and empowerment.
