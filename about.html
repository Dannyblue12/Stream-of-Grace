Our Pastor

Meet the visionary leader behind Stream of Grace Chapel International

Pastor Peggy Lipton

Lead Pastor, Prophetess, Conference Speaker

Pastor Peggy Lipton is a dynamic and anointed prophetess, pastor, and conference speaker, dedicated to spreading the word of God and empowering believers worldwide. As the Lead Pastor of Stream of Grace Chapel International, she passionately serves her congregation and community with love, wisdom, and spiritual guidance.

With a strong online presence, Pastor Peggy connects with thousands through her social media platforms, sharing inspiring messages, prophetic insights, and practical teachings. Her ministry is marked by a deep commitment to spiritual growth, healing, and transformation.

Through her prophetic gift, Pastor Peggy brings hope, encouragement, and clarity to individuals and communities, helping them navigate life's challenges and discover their purpose. Under her leadership, Stream of Grace Chapel International has become a beacon of hope and a hub for spiritual growth.

Our Vision & Mission

The guiding principles that drive our ministry

Our Vision

To be a transformative spiritual community that empowers individuals to discover their purpose, develop their potential, and make a positive impact in the world through the love and power of Jesus Christ. We envision a church that bridges cultural divides, heals broken lives, and serves as a beacon of hope and restoration in our community and beyond.

Our Mission

To create an authentic community of believers who are passionate about worshipping God, growing in faith, serving others, and sharing the transformative message of the Gospel. We are committed to providing relevant biblical teaching, fostering genuine relationships, equipping people for ministry, and extending compassionate outreach to meet the spiritual and practical needs of our community.
